Tuning Pedro Pathing has four steps:

<Steps>
    <Step>
        <h3><Link href="/docs/tuning/localization">Localization</Link></h3>

        This allows for your robot to know its location on the field.
    </Step>
    <Step>
        <h3><Link href="/docs/tuning/automatic">Automatic Tuners</Link></h3>

        These tuners consist of running four OpModes that determine
        constants specific to your robot.
    </Step>
    <Step>
        <h3><Link href="/docs/tuning/pids">PID Tuners</Link></h3>

        This step is where you tune 3-6 PID controllers, translational,
        heading, and drive.
    </Step>
    <Step>
        <h3><Link href="/docs/tuning/centripetal">Centripetal Force
            Tuner</Link></h3>

        This step consists of tuning a constant that accounts for
        centripetal force.
    </Step>
</Steps>
